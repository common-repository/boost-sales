Upsell & Cross-sell relevant items when customers add to cart. Offer product bundles at discount price. Increase average order value.

== Description ==

Upselling & Cross-selling are 2 of the most effective selling strategies in both traditional brick & mortar commerce and the modern eCommerce. Amazon used to declare 35% of their total revenue was the direct result of Upselling & Cross-selling. WooCommerce Upsell & Cross-sell Popups plugin makes these powerful tactics possible, easy, and automated for WooCommerce merchants, to help you sell online more successfully.

Upsell & Cross-sell offers by this plugin display in form of interactive popups when customers are interested in an item. The popups encourage them to add more relevant items to cart or purchase many items in combo bundles at a total discount price. This way, it helps you sell more to each existing customer, and increase total revenue by at least 2-3 times.

= HIGHLIGHTS: =

*  Upsell & Cross sell offers encourage customers to add higher-valued items or purchase bundles to increase sales from any customers.
*  Excite customers with game design technique: motivate customers to spend a little more to reach a goal and receive sweet discount offers.
*  Last step up-sell offers on cart page to convert even more sales from paying customers.
*  Beautiful, modern and optimized UX/UI for easy use and maximum conversion rate.

= FEATURES: =

####UPSELL:

Offer customers higher-end alternatives or relevant products so you can increase even more sales from any orders.

- When customers add an item to cart, a popup will show up to suggest them a list of better options they may want to buy more or replace added items.
- If enabled by you, Boost Sales will automatically pick appropriate products to recommend for any orders and increase sales for you.

####CROSS-SELL:

Bundle products that go well together into an irresistible combo your customers love to buy.

- When customers view any product in the combo, a corner popup will appear with special effect to catch their attention and cross-sell the whole bundle.
- You can also offer discount coupons to promote your value deals even better, and significantly boost sales from any existing customers.

####SALES MOTIVATOR:

Gamify your offers to excite customers and make even MORE sales from their orders.

- Game design technique is applied in smart coupon popups that motivate your shoppers to add more products to cart in order to receive offers.
- Increase user engagement on your websites, motivate customers to spend more, come back & refer you to their friends

####LAST-STEP UPSELL:

Keep selling more for you on cart page!

- When customers click “Check Out” button in cart page, one last up-sell offer will show up to suggest them more items they might want to add before check out.
- These products are well-chosen based on their shopping behaviors and your sales history, which makes customers highly likely to purchase.

####QUICKVIEW:

Quick View helps to increase your conversion by letting customers preview a product and check out right in 1 screen, without having to browse many pages.

Plus, it includes the smart recommendation block right in the quick view popup to help you increase the average order value to the max!

####SMART RECOMMENDATIONS:

Using machine learning and big data technology, Boost Sales app automatically helps you suggest the right products to the right customers based on their browsing behaviors, which saves your time of creating custom offers, and increases your revenue.

####HOLIDAY THEMES COLLECTION:

Holiday season is the best sales time of the year! We want your store to be colorful, merry, and beautiful on the holiday season, thus we pre-design many holiday themes to help you decorate your store, impress your customers and grow your sales.

The collection includes themes for Halloween, Thanksgiving, Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas, New Year, Valentines,…and many more to be added!

####RESPONSIVE ON DESKTOP & MOBILE:

Offer popups are designed to be beautifully responsive on any desktop screens and mobile devices (tablets and smart phones). You can turn off the aplugin on mobile devices.

####TRANSLATION:

Default lannguage is English. We support text translation to any languages.
